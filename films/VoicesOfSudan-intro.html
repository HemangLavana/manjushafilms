Voices Of Sudan - Trailer

Director/Camera/Editor: Hemal Trivedi
The people of South Sudan make history on July 9th, 2011, as Southern Sudan celebrates independence as a new Nation. But the birth of this new nation may mean military invasion by powerful North Sudan. Voices of Sudan features the recollections of several Lost Boys of Sudan who now live and work in the United States as clergymen. The nine part series Voices of Sudan will present the hope of a better future for Sudan.
