Shabeena's Quest - Movie
In some parts of Pakistan getting an education is an act of defiance, but one woman is opening her school to all.

Manjusha Films LLC produced, directed and edited this documentary for Al-Jazeera English. This is the story of Shabeena, a remarkable school principal, and her quest to bring education to the children, particularly the young girls, living in the shadow of the Taliban in Pakistan.

Watch the full movie on Al-Jazeera's website: Shabeena's Quest
