DIL Schools In Pakistan - Promotional Video

Produced by: Manjusha Films
Director/Camera/Editor: Hemal Trivedi
Location: Pakistan
Year: 2010
Length: 8:49

In rural Pakistan DIL modern schools are bringing hope and opportunity to underprivileged Pakistani children. This film chronicles a day in life of children studying at DIL schools in rural Islamabad and looks at how their lives are transformed through education. For more information please visit DIL's website: dil.org .
