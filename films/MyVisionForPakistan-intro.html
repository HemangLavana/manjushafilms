Fear prevails in Pakistan, as the country battles the extremists who terrorize its people and wreak havoc across the land. Pakistan's very survival is at stake. In this environment of uncertainty, three secular schools in rural Pakistan choose to focus on their desire for a very different future. The feature documentary My Vision for Pakistan intimately follows four 5th-graders as they paint, sculpt and sketch their vision for their homeland in an inter-school art and craft competition.
