Beyond Mumbai - Hope And Healing - Trailer

Director/Camera/Editor: Hemal Trivedi
Location: India
Year: 2010
A five-part webseries for Odyssey Networks. The documentary follows Kia Scherr and survivors of the Mumbai terrorist attacks of 2008 as they returned to Mumbai. Sherr lost both her husband and daughter in the attack. She and other survivors work towards peace and celebrate the survivors. The series was nominated for Webby Award, won Interfaith Film Festival 2011 “Forgiveness” Award and Telly Silver Award.
