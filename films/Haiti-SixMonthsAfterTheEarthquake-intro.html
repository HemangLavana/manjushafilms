Haiti - Six Months After The Earthquake - Trailer

Editor: Hemal Trivedi
Haiti-Six Months After The Earthquake profiles a 76-year-old naked Jain monk as he teaches his students the secrets of the ancient science of attaining Nirvana. The filmmaker Hemal Trivedi spent months in the forests of India with the naked monks to learn about their esoteric sub-culture. Six months after the devastating earthquake that hit Haiti, people are still without basic necessities such as clean water. Odyssey Networks film crew travel to the island to see the relief projects, as well as the almost overwhelming amount of work still remaining.
